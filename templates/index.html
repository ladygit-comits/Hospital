    Cardiology

    Specializing in heart and cardiovascular health for diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of heart conditions.

    Our cardiology department offers a range of services, including diagnostics, non-invasive procedures, and personalized treatment plans for conditions like heart disease, arrhythmias, and hypertension. Our team is equipped with state-of-the-art technology to ensure the best care for our patients.

    Neurology

    Focused on disorders of the brain, spine, and nervous system for both acute and chronic conditions.

    Our neurology specialists provide expert care for patients experiencing neurological conditions such as epilepsy, multiple sclerosis, Parkinson's disease, and stroke. We prioritize comprehensive care with advanced diagnostic and therapeutic options to improve patients' quality of life.

    Hepatology

    Dedicated to liver, pancreas, and biliary system care, including diagnostics and therapeutic services.

    Our hepatology department provides treatment and management for liver diseases, including hepatitis, cirrhosis, and liver cancer. Our specialists work closely with patients to monitor and manage conditions to promote liver health and overall well-being.

    Pediatrics

    Providing specialized healthcare for infants, children, and adolescents in a nurturing environment.

    Our pediatric department offers comprehensive medical care, including preventive services, immunizations, and treatment for childhood illnesses. We focus on both physical and mental well-being to ensure healthy development in a child-friendly atmosphere.

    Ophthalmology

    Specializing in eye care, offering a range of diagnostic and surgical services for vision health.

    Our ophthalmology department provides comprehensive eye care, from routine eye exams to advanced treatments for conditions like glaucoma, cataracts, and retinal disorders. Our experienced team uses the latest technology to ensure optimal eye health for all patients.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Your health and well-being are important to us. Here are some answers to common questions.

    What services do you offer?

    We offer a wide range of medical services including outpatient consultations, inpatient care, surgeries, diagnostic testing, and health screenings. Our specialists cover various fields, from general medicine to more specialized areas like cardiology and pediatrics.

    How can I book an appointment?

    Booking an appointment is easy. You can either book online through our website or by calling our reception. For urgent matters, we also provide walk-in services during office hours.

    Do you accept insurance?

    Yes, we accept a wide range of health insurance providers. Please check with our customer service team to verify if your insurance is accepted, or feel free to inquire about out-of-pocket payment options.

    What should I bring to my appointment?

    When coming for an appointment, please bring your ID, medical history, insurance details (if applicable), and any medical reports or test results. This will help us provide you with the best care.

    How do I get my test results?

    You can receive your test results through our secure online portal, or you can pick them up in person from our reception. If you have any questions about your results, feel free to schedule a follow-up consultation with your doctor.

    Do you have emergency services?

    Yes, we have a 24/7 emergency department staffed with highly trained professionals. If you need immediate medical attention, please visit us at any time or call our emergency hotline.
